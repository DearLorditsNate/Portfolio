About me

My name is Nate and I am a developer for both financial causes and user-focused web applications. A graduate of the UCLA Extension Full-Stack Bootcamp I am versed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, jQuery, Git and GitHub.

The things I build focus on their essence. Whether it is a website, application, or program, I believe a project it works best when it does what it’s supposed to in a simple, uncluttered way.

I have over five years of experience in nonprofits building positive and genuine donor relationships through thoughtful, tactful, and empathic relationship management. I bring this same attention to detail and authentic communication to my web development.

I’m a quick learner, a clear communicator, supportive and collaborative with my team, and a leader amongst my peers. As a web developer, I prioritize attention to detail and have a passion for building products that make user's lives better.
